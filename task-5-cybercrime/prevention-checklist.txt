Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests (IMPORTANT)
- Remember: scanning a QR code sends money, not receives it (IMPORTANT)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious activity
